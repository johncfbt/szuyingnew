Are you GST registered? How do you collect the payment?
I am not GST registered. I usually invoice my clients at the end of each session.

How long does each session take?
A session typically lasts 50 minutes.

Do you offer sliding scale rates or other discount programmes?
No. There are however a number of opportunities for funding you can consider, such as insurance, Disability Allowance, Studylink, ACC or employment benefits.

Can anyone find out what's discussed in session?
All the sessions are strictly confidential. I can't disclose any details of our engagement without your prior consent. However, in case of the risk to self or others I amy need to work with you in session to report to the appropriate authorities to ensure your safety.

What is the FACT?
“Life is one big symptom generator! When you stop having symptoms, that’s the day you are declared DOA” Strosahl, Robinson and Gustavsson (2012; p.51)
The newly developed Focused Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (FACT) is the marriage between the GP care and psychology practice for the best interest of our client populations.
The FACT encourages a shift of thinking so that our clients can learn to become more flexible and resilent psycholigcally, and are able to commit into a preferred lifestyle that is in line with their core values and eventually will give them a greater sense of joy and wellbeing.
