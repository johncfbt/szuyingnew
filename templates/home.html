Clinical Psychology Consulting

A specialist practice in the areas of identity and developmental struggles, concerns of ethnic
and sexual or gender diversity including cultural integration, psychological trauma, and identity development.

Perhaps you're down or anxious - maybe you're unsure about the future, or just feel that you aren't living the life you were meant to lead. Whatever your needs may be, I am here to support.

Therapy is a process of growth and self-discovery. In my sessions, I promote holistic behavioral transformation by encouraging an action-focused/ value-driven attitudes and reactions.

Please note that I am a registered MediCare Provider in Australia. You may be eligible for a MediCare Rebate and/or private insurance if referred by your GP.

Available in English and Mandarin Chinese.

I CAN SUPPORT WITH

DEPRESSION

Low or unstable moods; feelings of low self-worth, helplessness; sadness; lack of motivation; mental injury, or grief and loss.

ANXIETY

Social anxiety; generalized anxiety; health anxiety; perfectionistic thinking; chronic depression and panic attacks.

OBSESSIVE AND COMPULSIVE TENDENCIES

Distressing intrusive and unwanted thoughts; repetitive or ritualistic behaviour; excessive consciousness or uncontrollable guilt.

DEVELOPMENTAL AND IDENTITY STRUGGLES

Psychosocial trauma, multiple identity; youth mental development; concerns of cultural and gender/sexual minority. These are the areas critically linked to reclaim “takatāpui” – “to be all of who we are.”
